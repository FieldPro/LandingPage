Track, Learn, Share:

Thriving with Plant Love

A platform that connects people who are passionate
about plants, enabling them to learn from each
other's knowledge and experiences.

Discover the Exciting Features

Plant Information

Leafy provides a vast database of plant species,
offering detailed information about the plants. Users
can access this information to expand their
knowledge and make informed decisions about their plants.

Community Interaction

Leafy provides a community platform where users
can connect with other plant enthusiasts nearby.
They can share their experience and their plant
progress with other users. This fosters a sense of
community and provides opportunities for learning
and collaboration.

Plant Growth Tracking

The app allows users to track the growth and
progress of their plants over time. They can take
photos, and add notes to monitor their plants'
development. This feature helps users assess the
health and performance of their plants.

Personalised Recommendations

Leafy offers personalised plant recommendations
based on user's preferences. By considering factors
like indoor or outdoor, sunlight exposure, and
watering needs, the app suggests suitable plant
options for users to explore.

Wishlist and Collections

Users can create wish lists of plants they desire to
have in the future or maintain a collection of plants
they currently own. This feature helps users stay
organized and keep track of their plant progress.

Notifications and Reminders

Leafy provides notifications and reminders to remind
the users to take pictures of the progress of their
plants and share with the community.
Why Leafy is the Must-Have App for Plant Lovers!
Experience the ultimate plant journey with Leafy, the essential mobile app for plant
enthusiasts. Discover any plant effortlessly and access comprehensive care
information to nurture them with confidence. Share your plant progress with a
